About Us

Welcome to our family-run eatery, where we pride ourselves on serving a delightful variety of freshly made waffles, each filled with an array of tasty and unique fillings. Our passion for food and family shines through in every bite, bringing you not only delicious waffles but also a taste of traditional Filipino street food. Each dish is crafted with love and care, ensuring an authentic and memorable dining experience that transports you to the vibrant streets of the Philippines. Join us and become part of our extended family as we share our culinary heritage with you.
